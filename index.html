WHO ARE WE?

A group of nine students from one of the nation's best high schools, Thomas Jefferson High School of Science and Technology, located in Northern Virginia

 

Sricharan Bachu


My name is Sricharan Bachu, but my friends call me Charan. I am currently a sophomore at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. I competed in FIRST Lego League for one year, mentored my brothers team for two years. In my free time I play FIFA on the PS3 and play soccer with my friends. This year, in FTC, I would like to further my knowledge of robots gained through competing in FIRST. In addition, I would like to apply my programming skills build an autonomous robot for the competition.

 

Arko Bhattacharyya


My name is Arko Bhattacharyya, but my friends call me Alex. I am currently a sophomore at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. I started robotics in 2010. I have lots of experience with First Lego League. This year I plan to apply new building skills I have gained from school and also gain new skills directly from FTC. My focus is building a highly engineered robot but I also enjoy all the other aspects of FTC. I can’t wait to show in FTC tournaments what our team is capable of accomplishing.

 

Tanvi Haldankar


My name is Tanvi Haldankar, and I am currently a sophomore at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. This is my first year doing any robotics challenge and I can’t wait to see what we can accomplish. I absolutely love anything to do with computer science and applications of it in the real world. Some of my hobbies include playing piano, playing soccer, and being a Girl Scout! This year, in FTC, I hope to get more experience with building parts of the robot and applying what I currently know about computer science!

 

Shwetark Patel


My name is Shwetark Patel, and I am a sophomore at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. This is my first year doing anything with FIRST, and I’m very excited to get started. I’ve been programming for seven years using a variety of programming languages, and have had a lot of fun developing open source apps, competing in hackathons, and developing websites. Last year, I made the USACO camp after placing well in the monthly USACO contests. I’m very excited to apply my programming skills to real world robotics challenges through FTC.

 

Emily Quan


My name is Emily Quan and I am a sophomore at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. This is my first year working with FIRST, and I’m excited to learn more about robotics and technology. I love computer science and mathematics, and I can’t wait to apply these to robotics! In my spare time I enjoy running cross country, reading, playing piano, and rock climbing.

 

Rahul Rajan


My name is Rahul Rajan, and I am a sophomore at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. I am very passionate about STEM and have taken part and won awards in STEM competitions including FIRST Lego League, Technology Student Association, National Science Bowl, John Hopkins Science Bowl, Toshiba Exploravision. My team was placed first and received the Champions trophy in First Lego League Regionals in 2009 & 2010. I also won the William T Reed Most competitive Technosphere participant in Virginia in 2014. These competitions enhanced my problem solving and collaboration skills. However, most importantly, it increased my passion for Robotics and STEM in general. I have also have gained a lot of experience in CAD, 3D printing and have strong programming skills. This will be my first year doing FTC and I hope to use the skills I gained from FIRST and other competitions to design and build a successful robot and spread my passion for STEM in my community.
